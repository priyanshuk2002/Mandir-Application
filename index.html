About Us

Our Sacred Temple

ॐ

Shri Rameshwer Dham Mandir

Located in Vaishali Eligense Society, Gandhi Path West, Vaishali Nagar, Jaipur, our mandir is dedicated to Lord Shiva. The sanctum houses a beautifully adorned Shiva Lingam, where devotees gather for daily prayers and special occasions.

Under the guidance of Shri Rameshwar Dham Charitable Trust, we are committed to serving the community through spiritual activities and charitable initiatives.

  • 🕉️
    Daily Worship

    Regular puja and aarti for Lord Shiva

  • 🤝
    Charitable Trust

    Community service through our registered trust

  • 📍
    Vaishali Nagar, Jaipur

    Easily accessible in West Jaipur
